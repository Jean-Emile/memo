Simple and secure file synchronization for professionals

Infinit Drive benefits from the simplicity of a consumer product, the security and scalability of enterprise-class technologies allowing one to choose where the files will eventually be located.

Control the location of your data

When creating a drive, you can choose where your files will actually be stored:
•  in your country
•  or even on a specific hosting provider

Note that unlike Dropbox, Infinit Drive does not use your local storage capacity unless you want it to.

Store and access your files

Once your drive available, you can store, access and even stream files as if they were stored on your local hard disk.

Unlike Google Drive with which it is difficult to understand who sees what file/folder, Infinit maintains a single and coherent hierarchical view of the files for everyone.

Increase your productivity

Infinit Drive supports a variety of cloud services that can be integrated for you to work the way you want.
