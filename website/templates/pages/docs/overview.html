Overview

The Infinit Storage Platform allows for the creation of scalable, resilient and secure storage infrastructure for modern and container environments.


Infinit benefits from several advantages over the centralized and distributed systems commonly used.

Control

Secure

All the files stored are cut into chunk and every chunk is encrypted (RSA/AES-256) with a unique key.

Control

Control the localisation of your data by selecting the storage resources/providers involved in the infrastructure.

Reliable

Every block of every file is dynamically replicated several times (configurable by the administrator) in order to ensure durability and availability of the data.

Fault Tolerant

Detects failures and automatically adapts the number of replicas in order to guarantee the reliability of the data.

Scalability

Flexible

Manage your needs in terms of storage capacity by increasing/decreasing (scale up/down) contributions from existing providers and/or adding/removing (scale out/in) storage resources without interrupting the service.

Scalable

With a peer-to-peer foundation, Infinit does not suffer single points of failure or bottlenecks.

High Performance

Benefit from a high-throughput UDP-based network protocol for faster data retrieval.

Adaptability

In the event of a failure, the system heals itself by automatically adjusting the number of replicas.

Administration

Migration

Easily migrate your data from one provider to another whenever you need without any service interruption.

Access Control

Decide who can read/edit files and folders through fine-grained access control capabilities.

Affordable

No need to acquire specific hardware/appliance or setup/maintain a complicated server-side installation.

Versioning

Browse through the different versions of a file and rollback if necessary.

Interface

Natural

Access your files on any device, from anywhere at any time through a native file system interface.

Interoperability

Runs on your dearest operating system: macOS, Windows, Linux, iOS and Android

On Demand

Access/stream the files on-demand without the need to store the files locally or download them before.
