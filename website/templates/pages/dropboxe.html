Infinit — Project Dropboxe

Access all your files without sync but with real encryption

Build scalable and secure storage infrastructures by aggregating local storage and various cloud storages such as Amazon S3 or Dropbox.



Real encryption

With Dropbox, only the communications are encrypted while the blocks are stored in plain form on the servers.

But Project Dropboxe provides a storage solution that both encrypts in transit and at rest so that a block of data cannot be decrypted by a potentially malicious storage server. It helps not having your passwords leaked around the Internet.

Control the location of your data

When creating your infrastructure, you can choose where your files will actually be stored in your country or even on a specific hosting provider.

Store and access your files

Store, access and even stream files as if they were stored on your local hard disk. It's a "revolutionary" new way to access all your files.
