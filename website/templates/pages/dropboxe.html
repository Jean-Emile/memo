Access all your files without sync but with real encryption

Build scalable and secure storage infrastructures by aggregating local storage and various cloud storages such as Amazon S3 or Dropbox.



Real encryption

With Dropbox, only the communications are encrypted while the blocks are stored in plain form on the servers.

Infinit provides a storage solution that both encrypts in transit and at rest so that a block of data cannot be decrypted by a potentially malicious storage server.

Control the location of your data

When creating a drive, you can choose where your files will actually be stored in your country or even on a specific hosting provider.

Note that unlike Dropbox, Infinit Drive does not use your local storage capacity unless you want it to.

Store and access your files

Once your drive opened, you can store, access and even stream files as if they were stored on your local hard disk.
