The Team

We believe that everyone has the right to privacy and peace-of-mind in particular when it comes to files, being personal or professional.

We started working on a file sharing product as a demonstration of the power and flexibility of the technology we have developed. After 3 years of hard work, the Infinit filesystem is finally available to developers and administrators around the world.

Antony Méchin

Retired Carnivore

Antony has recently made a commitment to be vegetarian, at least 8 days per month. He enjoys taking long walks on Southridge Beach and on occasion stops for a beer at the Prancing Pony Inn. In his free time, he enjoys the company of dwarves and elves.

Baptiste Fradin

Local Punk Star

An up and coming heavy metal singer until 2008, Baptiste became vocally incapacitated when he was tackled by his biggest fan, Meatball, during a concert in Edinburgh. Lacking a harsh vocal style, he turned to the opera where he worked as coat room attendant until founding Infinit.

Chris Crone

Toofer Wun

In 2010, Chris stumbled upon a Velib’ in Johannesburg, South Africa. Tired of surfing and mangos, and driven by a need to live in a city with a gratuitously large public bike system, Chris crossed Africa by bike to achieve his dream of living in Paris.

Gaëtan Rochel

Ze German

Gaëtan – what the hell kind of name is that? Gaëtan was born in Moselle, a French region often invaded by Germans. In 2009, he founded an electronic music movement to bring the two countries even closer together. He was not yet aware of the existence of the EU.

Julien Quintard

Papa Pastis

Four years ago, Julien applied to be on the French equivalent of the Jersey Shore, but failed miserably during the interviews. With his dreams in shambles, he left for the University of Cambridge to write his thesis on a worldwide storage infrastructure.

Matthieu Nottale

Crazy Cat Lady

Matthieu has the unique story of being raised in the countryside by 3 cats: Marc, Luc and Jean. His distinctive childhood spurred his interests in feline automation. His most recent feat of engineering consists of a robotic tabby that can amazingly, stretch, purr and sleep.

Patrick Perlmutter

Faux Bobo

A lover of both freedom and baguettes, Patrick chose to give up red, white and blue in favor of rouge, blanc and bleu – especially the bleu. The rouge and the blanc aren’t so bad either.

Quentin Hocquet

Pippi Longstocking

Inspired by Buddhist monks, Quentin decided to grow his hair out starting in 1996. He launched a line of beauty products for fans of hardcore metal. Overwhelmed by his success, he went on to build the world’s first automated, robotic hair stylist.
